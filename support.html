How do I play HumanKnot?
HumanKnot is a puzzle game where you need to untangle interconnected elements. Tap and drag to move pieces around and solve the puzzle by separating all the connected elements.
Is HumanKnot free to play?
Yes, HumanKnot is completely free to download and play. There are no in-app purchases or advertisements.
Does HumanKnot work offline?
Yes! HumanKnot works completely offline. You don't need an internet connection to play the game.
What devices are supported?
HumanKnot is available for iPhone and iPad running iOS 13.0 or later. The app is optimized for both portrait and landscape orientations.
How do I reset a puzzle?
Look for the reset button (usually a circular arrow icon) in the game interface. This will restart the current puzzle from the beginning.
Are there different difficulty levels?
Yes, HumanKnot features multiple difficulty levels to challenge players of all skill levels, from beginner to expert.
How do I report a bug?
Please email us at tiffany.lohfink@gmail.com with the subject line "Bug Report" and include details about what happened and what device you're using.
Can I suggest new features?
Absolutely! We welcome feature suggestions. Please email us at tiffany.lohfink@gmail.com with your ideas.

üîß Troubleshooting

App Crashes

If the app crashes:

  1. Close the app completely (swipe up from bottom and swipe the app away)
  2. Restart your device
  3. Reopen the app
  4. If the problem persists, please contact support

Performance Issues

If the app runs slowly:

  1. Close other apps running in the background
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you have enough free storage space
  4. Update to the latest version of iOS

Touch Response Issues

If touch controls aren't responding properly:

  1. Clean your screen
  2. Remove any screen protectors that might interfere
  3. Restart the app
  4. Check if your device's touch screen works in other apps
